Scale 0–5 · 1 Novice → 3 Competent → 5 Expert

Session flow: first third — understand where they are · middle third — find the single highest-leverage gap · final third — agree on one concrete next step for this week.
Opening / Context ›
  • What do you currently work on, and what's your role?
  • How long have you been in this field?
  • What brought you to this mentoring session today?
Current Skill Level ›
  • On a scale of 1–10, how confident do you feel in your core skills? Why that number?
  • What part of your work feels easy and natural to you now?
  • What part still makes you slow down or feel uncertain?
  • Can you walk me through a recent project you're proud of?
  • What's a problem you got stuck on recently, and how did you handle it?
Work & Habits ›
  • What does a typical work week look like for you?
  • How do you currently learn new things — courses, docs, building, mentors?
  • What tools or technologies do you use daily?
  • Do you work alone or in a team, and how does that affect your growth?
Goals & Improvement ›
  • Where do you want to be in 6 months? In 2 years?
  • What specific skill, if you improved it, would change the most for you?
  • What's been holding you back from improving that so far?
  • How much time per week can you realistically invest in growth?
  • How will you know you've improved — what does success look like?
Closing ›
  • What's one thing you'd like to walk away from this session with?
  • Who or what supports your growth right now? Where are the gaps?
